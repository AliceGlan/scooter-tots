Welcome to Scooter Tots!


Our Mission


To provide a welcoming and inclusive group that benefits both tots and their parents. To provide our tots with space to develop their physical and social development, and reduce parental isolation.


Benefits for Tots


  • Getting some fresh air
  • Making new friends
  • Lots of space to scoot or ride
  • Learn and develop physical skills
  • Lots of opportunties for play

Benefits for you


  • Getting out of the house
  • Meeting new people
  • Reduce the feelings of isolation
  • An opportunity to speak to other adults!
  • Bonding time with your tot

Do not fear!


Attending a group can be daunting for some parents and carers, it has certainly been a barrier for me in the past. Please drop me a message and we can talk through any fears you may have about attending. We offer a warm and welcoming group for anyone needing to get out of the house and get some fresh air.


Rules of play


We ask that you bring your tot with their own scooter or other ride-on-wheeled toy, and accompanying safety gear.
